"Last of the (Lady)bugs" Caprese

  1. Wash basil and cherry tomatoes
  2. Slice mozzarella cheese
  3. Slice cherry tomatoes lengthwise
  4. Layer mozzarella, basil, cherry tomato
  5. Use balsamic glaze to draw line and dots of ladybug

Mint Chocolate Pretzel Stick (Seasons)

  1. Line a tray with parchment paper
  2. Melt ¼ cup choco chips (30s microwave intervals, stir in between)
  3. Mix in ¼ tsp peppermint extract
  4. Dip one side of the pretzel in the melted chocolate
  5. Lay pretzel on parchment paper and repeat
  6. Place in the fridge for 15 minutes to set
